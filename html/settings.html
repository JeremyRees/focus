Settings

Use this page to add or remove websites to or from the lists below.

  • The first group is for sites you can't trust yourself with; while in 'focus' mode*, they'll be outright blocked.
  • If you navigate to a site in the second group, we'll give you a little nudge, and you can judge whether it's worth your time. Recommended for gray-area sites that can be useful but can also get you distracted easily.
  • And of course, we humans can only stay focused for so long without losing it, which is what the third group is for. When you need to take a breath, you'll be free to visit these sites, but your time there will be limited to ten minutes.

As far as formatting, we're not strict. As long as the pattern you input will appear in a site's url, it will work fine. It can be anything from "https://www.google.com" to just "twitter"—or even "wIKIpEdiA.ORg"; it's not case sensitive, so feel free to go crazy!

*(Which, by the way, you can toggle with the shortcut Ctrl+Shift+F)
